Mapping the Global Gap in Interventional Radiology Access for Postpartum Hemorrhage

This project maps the global gap between postpartum hemorrhage (PPH) burden and interventional radiology (IR) access. Using WHO data and IR workforce data from literature and direct outreach, we performed a geospatial analysis of mortality versus access.

IR data were available for only 22 countries, revealing a critical data gap. The pattern is clear: the highest-burden regions have the least IR access.
